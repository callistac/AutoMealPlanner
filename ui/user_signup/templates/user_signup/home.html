Tell us about yourself

Create a free account with us and input your meal preferences, dietary restrictions, and weekly budget. We will try our best to generate meals for you based on your preferences and will keep your weekly meals within your budget.

Get meal suggestions

We recommend random recipes based on your preferences all from allrecipes.com. We go a step further and automatically generate weekly grocery lists for you based on those recipes as well as let you download that grocery list to your computer.

Give us feedback

Don't like one or more of the randomly generated meals? Have no fear, you aren't stuck with the recipes we generate for you! We give you the option to replace recipes you dont like. You can give us feedback on why you deselected those recipes as well as rate recipes from past weeks so that we can learn what you like.
