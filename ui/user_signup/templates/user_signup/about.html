What is this tool I have stumbled upon??


Humans are inherently lazy creatures. Meal prepping for the week can require a lot of work, more than you'd probably want to put in considering your hectic, crazy life at UChicago--it's just one more thing you have to worry about on top of your SOSC essay and math pset and RSO meeting and research work.

And that's why we created the Automatic Meal Planner, a tool that generates random recipes for you based on your preferences, such as any dietary restrictions or budget limitations. If you dislikes a given recipe, you can deselect it from your weekly view and tell us why so we will only show that meal again if appropriate based on your response. You can also view your past recipes and rate them so we can get a sense of what you like, and also automatically create a grocery list for your weekly recipes.

All you have to do is sign up and tell us a little about your preferences, and your meals will be ready with the click of a button!
